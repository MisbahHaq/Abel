A RADICAL
APPROACH

Behind every Abel fragrance is a radical approach to how we operate. In a fragrance world shrouded by secrecy, we’re always transparent.

In a culture fuelled by hype and excess, we favour the long view and curbing our environmental footprint. And in our use of biotechnology, guided by the artistry of our master perfumer, we create long-lasting 100% natural fragrances that eliminate the need for petrochemical-derived synthetic ingredients.

OUR STORY

Founded by former winemaker Frances Shoemack in 2013, Abel first emerged in Amsterdam and is now headquartered in Wellington, New Zealand. Operating across oceans, we work closely with master perfumer Isaac Sinclair and perfumer Dr Fanny Grau to create forward-thinking natural fragrances, made in good conscience.
